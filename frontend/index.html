3. Högpåverkande suboptimaliteter (Sub-optimalities)

4. Banbrytande lösningsstruktur (Groundbreaking Solution)

Instead of simple "if/then" rules, we use a tripartite AI architecture:
• Optimization (SciPy) for immediate tactical reflexes.
• Game-Theory AI (MCTS) for strategic future-proofing and reserve management.
• Generative AI (Gemini 2.5) for translating complex mathematical assignments into readable SITREPs.
