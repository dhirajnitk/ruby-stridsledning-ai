# VIDEO PROMPTS — SCENARIO 06: HYPERSONIC RAID
**Theme:** Time-critical hypersonic PGM attack — THAAD-only engagement window, 40-second decision cycle
**Duration:** 8 clips × ~7 seconds = ~56 seconds total  
**Style:** High-velocity physics, yellow threat glow, extreme speed particle trails, time-pressure HUD

---

## CLIP 01 — HYPERSONIC DETECTION — LATE WARNING
**Prompt:**  
Dark early warning radar display. A blazing yellow track materialises at 800 km altitude, descending at extreme speed. The radar track is almost vertical, moving faster than the radar sweep can fully resolve. HUD: "TRACK FOXTROT-1 — HYPERSONIC PGM — SPEED: 7,200 m/s — MACH 21 — WARNING: ENGAGEMENT WINDOW CRITICAL." A countdown timer appears immediately: "THAAD WINDOW: 38 SECONDS." The screen edge flares yellow.

## CLIP 02 — CORTEX EMERGENCY DECISION
**Prompt:**  
CORTEX-1 command console in emergency mode — red border pulsing. Chain-of-thought text scrolls urgently: "HYPERSONIC DETECTED — ONLY THAAD CAN ENGAGE — ALL OTHER EFFECTORS: OUT OF ENVELOPE — THAAD INVENTORY: 8 — ASSIGNING: 2 × THAAD — UTILITY: 3,200 — DEADLINE: 34 SECONDS." Neural confidence bar at 94%. The assignment matrix flashes and locks instantly. "THAAD × 2 — FIRE AUTHORISATION AUTOMATIC."

## CLIP 03 — THAAD EMERGENCY LAUNCH
**Prompt:**  
Extreme urgency shot: a THAAD launcher fires two interceptors in rapid succession, only 3 seconds apart. The missiles launch almost horizontally, arcing steeply upward into the exoatmosphere. Exhaust columns are massive. The surrounding terrain is snowy Nordic forest. The camera vibrates with the shock wave. HUD countdown: "WINDOW: 22s." Warning: "NO FALLBACK SYSTEM — THAAD IS SOLE INTERCEPT OPTION."

## CLIP 04 — HYPERSONIC TARGET IN FLIGHT
**Prompt:**  
Cinematic side-view of the hypersonic missile in flight. The weapon is a sleek wedge shape, surrounded by a glowing plasma sheath — bright yellow-white from the heat of hypersonic compression. It leaves a faint orange trail behind it. Below: the blue-white curve of the Earth's upper atmosphere. HUD: "PLASMA SHEATH ACTIVE — RADAR RESOLUTION DEGRADED — TRACK UNCERTAINTY: ±12km." The object moves unnervingly fast.

## CLIP 05 — EXOATMOSPHERIC INTERCEPT ATTEMPT
**Prompt:**  
3D kinetic view: two cyan THAAD kill vehicles close on the hypersonic track at high relative velocity. The intercept geometry is extremely tight — closing from angles at near-simultaneous arrival. HUD: "INTERCEPT 1 — 4s / INTERCEPT 2 — 7s." The track weaves slightly due to plasma-sheath uncertainty. The first kill vehicle detonates nearby — shrapnel cloud. The second closes the remaining distance.

## CLIP 06 — INTERCEPT CONFIRMED
**Prompt:**  
Brilliant explosion in the upper atmosphere. The hypersonic missile disintegrates — superheated fragments cascade outward, each leaving its own glowing trail. The fireball is white-gold, fading quickly in the thin air. HUD: "FOXTROT-1 DESTROYED — THAAD DIRECT HIT — ENGAGEMENT WINDOW USED: 100% — TIME REMAINING: 2 SECONDS." The theater display shows the threat track disappearing. Relief visual: green status bar fills.

## CLIP 08 — LESSON: THAAD IS THE ONLY OPTION

Split-screen infographic style. Left panel labeled "HYPERSONIC — NO THAAD": red track reaching defended asset, explosion, red failure status.
