## 2. Key Metric Definitions

### **Tactical Pk (Efficiency)**
Measures the probability of a successful intercept for every fired SAM. A high Pk indicates superior ammunition management and precision targeting.

### **Strategic Success (Survival)**
Measures the percentage of full scenario waves where **zero** threats breached the defense perimeter. This is the primary safety KPI for national defense.
